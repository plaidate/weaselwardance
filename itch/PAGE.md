# Weasel War Dance — itch.io page copy

**Tagline:** Eight mustelids. Eight songs. One hypnotic war dance — hit the beat, catch dinner.

## Description

Stoats and weasels really do dance to mesmerize their prey — a frenzied
tumble of hops, flips and spins that leaves a rabbit too baffled to run.
Weasel War Dance gives that trick a beat. Cues stream into the hit ring;
answer them with hops, pounces, dooks and crank spins, and your parametric
mustelid dances while the prey's eyes spiral. Every song is a step
sequencer driven by the game clock itself, so audio and judgment can never
drift apart.

Work up the family from a least weasel twitching at a vole to a giant
otter raving at a caiman: eight dancers, eight original chiptune songs
from 100 to 158 BPM, each with its own scenery, prey and signature moves.
Mesmerize each victim at least 60 percent to catch it and unlock the next,
larger dancer. Chase S ranks, guard your combo, and mind the golden rule
of the war dance: never mash off the beat.

## Features

- 8 playable mustelids, least weasel to giant otter, each a procedurally
  drawn dancer with species markings (stoat tail-tip, ferret mask,
  badger stripe...)
- 8 original clock-driven step-sequencer songs, 100–158 BPM — zero-drift
  by construction
- Full move set: d-pad hops, A pounces, B dooks, and crank-powered spin
  sections
- Prey that visibly succumbs: spiral eyes, swaying, hearts — and flinches
  when you stumble
- PERFECT/GOOD judgment, combos, flub penalties for off-beat mashing
- S/A/B/C/D ranks with best scores saved per song
- One sock. It can be mesmerized. Ferrets know this.

## Controls

- **D-pad** — hop left / right, jump, crouch
- **A** — pounce
- **B** — dook
- **Crank** — spin sections: keep it turning until the coil runs out
